About Us

Computer games are loaded with incredible graphics, but the ultimate gaming experience requires the right equipment. When it comes to multiplayer, simulations, or first-person shooters, there’s no better way to feel like you’re in the middle of the action than with a high-end computer, laptop, or notebook to call your own. WaldorfPC specializes in designing and building notebooks, laptops, and desktop computers to suit any personal sense of style. If you are ready to buy your own gaming computer, explore our inventory to find the perfect laptop or PC to get started.
